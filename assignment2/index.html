Visualization 1: Discover prostitution against vehicle theft in SF (2013-2015)

Take a look at Prostitution vs Crime Theft in the area of San Francisco in the responsive scatterplot below. The main question we want to answer here is "How both crimes developed over time?"

Features:

  • Switch between years 2013 and 2015 to get an idea how crime developed in each district
  • Hover over circle to see which district it belongs to
  • The size of each circle reflects the total number of crimes committed in the selected district and year

Visualization 2: Discover temporal developments of crime

This visualization aims at temporal patterns of crimes across 24h of the day. The main question we want to answer is "If we knew that a crime is about to happen in SF, at which time it happens most likely?"

Features:

  • Choose the focus category from the dropdown
  • Hover over bar to see how many crimes of this type were committed at the given hour
  • Each bar reflects the probability of a crime to happen at the given hour
